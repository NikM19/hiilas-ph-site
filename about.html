My Story

My name is Anastasia, and I live in Helsinki. I see the world with love and want to share my vision through the lens. Love comes in many forms — for yourself, your partner, your family, your pet, or your work.

I especially love intimate weddings, where the focus is on just the two of you. I capture feelings, intimacy, and love. My photos are like movies — you want to rewatch them, print them, and share them with your loved ones. Clients often tell me they see themselves differently through my perspective, and many come back again and again.
